🌌 About Saturn

Saturn is the second largest planet and is famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock particles. It is so light that it could float in water if a giant ocean existed. Saturn has dozens of moons including Titan, which has lakes of liquid methane.

✨ Fun Facts

  • Most spectacular rings in the solar system
  • Less dense than water
  • Strong winds up to 1800 km/h
  • Titan may support life chemicals
