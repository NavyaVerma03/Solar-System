🌌 About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the Red Planet due to iron oxide on its surface. It has the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons, and a giant canyon called Valles Marineris. Scientists believe Mars once had water and may have supported life in the past.

  • Gravity is 38% of Earth
  • Humans may live on Mars in future
