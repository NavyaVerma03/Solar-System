🌌 About Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and the hottest planet in the solar system. Its thick toxic atmosphere traps heat causing an extreme greenhouse effect. The Sun rises in the west and sets in the east on Venus because it rotates backwards!

✨ Fun Facts

  • Venus is called Earth's twin
  • A day on Venus is longer than its year
  • Acid rain exists in its atmosphere
  • Brightest planet visible from Earth
