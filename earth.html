🌌 About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It has liquid water, oxygen-rich atmosphere and perfect temperature conditions. Earth’s magnetic field protects life from harmful solar radiation.

✨ Fun Facts

  • Earth is not a perfect sphere
  • 70% of Earth is covered with water
  • Only planet known with life
  • Has a protective ozone layer
