🌌 About Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and is made mostly of gas. It has a massive storm called the Great Red Spot that has been raging for over 300 years. Jupiter acts like a space shield, protecting inner planets from comets and asteroids.

✨ Fun Facts

  • Jupiter could fit 1300 Earths inside it
  • Shortest day among all planets
  • Has faint rings
  • Strongest gravity after Sun
