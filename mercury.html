🌌 About Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the fastest planet in our solar system. It has no atmosphere, so temperatures change extremely between day and night. A year on Mercury is shorter than its day!

✨ Fun Facts

  • Mercury has the biggest temperature variation
  • You would weigh less on Mercury
  • Sun looks 3 times bigger from Mercury
  • No seasons exist on Mercury
