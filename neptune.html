🌌 About Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and is known for its deep blue color. It has the fastest winds in the solar system reaching over 2,000 km/h. Neptune was discovered using mathematical predictions before it was seen in a telescope.

✨ Fun Facts

  • Strongest winds in solar system
  • Deep blue methane atmosphere
  • Takes 165 years to orbit Sun
  • Discovered in 1846
