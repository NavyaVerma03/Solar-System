🌌 About Sun

The Sun is a massive glowing ball of hot plasma at the center of the solar system. It provides light and heat that makes life possible on Earth. Its gravity holds all planets in orbit around it. Without the Sun, no life could exist.

✨ Fun Facts

  • Contains 99.86% of solar system mass
  • Light takes 8 minutes to reach Earth
  • Made mostly of hydrogen and helium
  • Will become a red giant in future
