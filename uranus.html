🌌 About Uranus

Uranus is unique because it rotates on its side, making its seasons extreme. It appears blue-green due to methane gas in its atmosphere. A single season on Uranus lasts 21 Earth years.

✨ Fun Facts

  • Coldest planet in solar system
  • Rotates sideways
  • Has faint rings
  • Discovered in 1781
